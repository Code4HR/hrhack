Risks & Challenges

Projects on Civic Starter are community-driven. This means that when a project petition meets it signature and funding goals, we work with local government liaisons get the project green lighted and underway as soon as possible. Due to the differences in how each municipality works, Civic Starter's efforts to pushes your project to the front of the line may be met with locality-specific challenges such as complying with ordinances, zoning code regulations, and other laws.
