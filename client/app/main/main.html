How It Works

Encourages volunteerism and even create jobs

Projects that require volunteer hours can collect pledges on the project profile page.

Enable local governments to take action

Civicstarter has relationships with local governments and media outlets to not only get the word out, but also move larger projects through the legislation process.

Projects are funded by the people of a community

Once projects collect the necessary number of signatures the project is eligible for funding. The power is now in the hands of the community members.

Gain support through petition signatures

Users create petitions to start projects or purchase resources for their community. A determined number of signatures is necessary to move a project forward.
